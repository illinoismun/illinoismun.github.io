Welcome to Model United Nations at Illinois

It is with great pleasure, as Secretary-General, that I welcome you to Model United Nations at Illinois! We are thrilled to host our 27th annual MUNI Conference for high school students this coming Spring 2025. Building upon that momentum, the Illinois MUN team is dedicated to providing an exceptional experience for delegates at every stage of their MUN journey. I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to both our current members and alumni for their incredible commitment to making this conference a reality.

With our delegates' experience as our top priority, we have developed numerous resources and policies to create a safe and engaging environment for all participants. At MUNI, we are passionate about offering a diverse range of committees on contemporary and historical topics in global politics, designed to challenge delegates from all backgrounds to think critically about the issues that have shaped and continue to shape our world.

From dynamic crisis scenarios to detailed judging criteria, every aspect of our MUNI XXVII committees is crafted to foster growth and improvement, regardless of the delegates' experience levels. We believe a successful delegate is adaptable, professional, and above all, collaborative. We are confident that all participants will find this conference both stimulating and rewarding.
